About me

Dr. Princy Randhawa is serving as an Assistant Professor (Senior Scale) in the Department of Mechatronics University at Manipal University Jaipur, Jaipur, for the last 7 years. She has over 9 years of teaching and industrial experience. She has completed his Ph.D. in February 2021 with a specialization in Machine Learning in Wearable Technology. Her fields of interest lie in research topics like Control Systems, Machine learning. She has published original more than 20 research papers at various international conferences and journals in the finest area of her research. She has published 8 papers in SCI journals out of which 3 are Q1 journals. She is having a good publication record with 2 patents, and 1 copyright on her research. She is a professional member of the International Society of Automation (ISA) and IEEE. She is also an active member of various NGOs and conducted various cancer awareness and woman sanitation hygiene programs in rural colleges.
